ChupAds Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Summary

ChupAds is a browser extension that blocks advertisements and trackers on websites you visit. Processing happens on your device. We do not operate a backend that receives your browsing history.

Data we access

  • Filter lists — If you enable updates, the extension downloads public ad-blocking lists from EasyList, EasyPrivacy, and other configured URLs.
  • Storage — Settings, filter data, and session block counters are stored locally in Chrome storage on your device.
  • Tabs — Used only to show stats for the active tab and reload pages when you toggle blocking.

Data we do not collect

  • No account or login
  • No analytics SDK
  • No sale of personal data

Third-party filter sources

Updated lists are maintained by third-party projects (e.g. EasyList). Their privacy practices apply to those downloads.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer listed on the Chrome Web Store listing for ChupAds.
