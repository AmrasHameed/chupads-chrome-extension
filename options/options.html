Privacy

ChupAds blocks ads and trackers locally on your device. Filter lists may be downloaded from the sources above when updates are enabled. We do not collect personal data, browsing history, or sell user information.

Stats shown in the popup (blocked counts) are stored only in your browser session storage and are not sent to any server.
