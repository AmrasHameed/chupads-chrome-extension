Welcome to ChupAds

ChupAds is installed and blocking ads. Pin it to your toolbar so you can open stats and settings in one click.

Chrome does not allow extensions to pin themselves automatically — you need to pin ChupAds once (takes a few seconds).
1

Open the extensions menu

Click the puzzle piece 🧩 icon in the top-right of Chrome (next to your profile picture).

2

Pin ChupAds

Find ChupAds – Block Ads & Trackers in the list and click the pin icon so it stays on the toolbar.

3

Use the icon anytime

Click the ChupAds icon to see blocked counts, toggle blocking, or pick elements to hide.
